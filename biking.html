biking

For two months of summer 2021, I biked the East Coast Greenway, and sometimes drove along it. The Greenway hired me as a photographer (and general "rider") to advertise the NYC-to-Philadelphia bike ride, as well as to produce media content for the trail in general.

I took it upon myself to write creatively along the way. My thoughts, disjoint and ungainly, are in the "blog" section.

I was also privileged to try some amazing restaurants, coffee shops, and breweries along the coast. In the "justin's michelin guide to the east coast" section, you'll find rankings of the places that made me the happiest.
